Rules of Robots

In Rules of Robots you and your friends fight against each other trying to escape the bullet hell you put yourself into. Create the rules of combat and pick your battlefield.

Simple, fun and tweakable. This game is intense and engaging. Quick to learn and get your friends into. It innovates in the very, dynamic, tight levels, and with a ruleset you can tailor to your preferences.

About the project

This game started out as student project. In 2020 me and my team of four decided to take the game further, and together started the company Sharp Raccoon. The game is now under the development phase, and is aimed to realise on Nintendo Switch (TBA).

My responsibilities for the game is being in charge of the art pipeline, and contribute to asset development

Main tasks

  • Animation
  • 3D Modeling 
  • Shaders
  • VFX
  • Texturing
  • Design
