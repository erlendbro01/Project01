The Second Ring

Student project

My task was to create all the visuals in this narrative walking simulator. These tasks included generating terrain, foliage, shaders, 3D modelling and texturing, as well as lighting.

Plants open up when the player is close and serves as waypoints

Foliage with custom wind shaders 

Terrain generator created for quick iteration

All textures are procedural
Cliff shader. The grass adjust based on mesh rotation, and the colors and wear can be adjusted for more variation.
